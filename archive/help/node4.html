UML reminder

UML stands for Unified Modeling Language. It's a standard notation to modelize problems and may be considered like the reference in object modelisation. Following diagrams have been found on the Rational Rose web site4. For a full UML reminder or to see the actual UML norm, see http://www.uml.org.
